Our Story

Gloss & Glow Resin Art was founded on a passion for creating beautiful, timeless pieces that celebrate life's special moments. What started as a creative hobby has blossomed into a thriving business dedicated to handcrafting premium resin art.

Our co-founders, Ashu Bansal and Mehak Mittal, bring together their expertise in design and craftsmanship to create unique, high-quality resin products. Every piece tells a story and brings joy to our customers.

Whether it's preserving a precious flower bouquet or creating a stunning custom resin piece, we're committed to exceptional quality and customer satisfaction.
