Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a custom order?

You can reach out via WhatsApp, email, or fill out our contact form. We recommend WhatsApp for the fastest response and to discuss your vision in detail!

What is the delivery timeframe?

Standard products ship within 3-5 business days. Custom orders and preservation services take 7-14 days. We'll confirm exact timelines once you place your order.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently, we operate within India. For international inquiries, please contact us via WhatsApp to discuss possibilities.

What is your return policy?

We guarantee quality on all our products. If you receive a damaged item, contact us immediately for a replacement or refund. Custom orders are non-returnable unless defective.

Can I request a custom design?

Absolutely! Custom orders are our specialty. Share your ideas, preferences, and requirements with us, and our team will bring your vision to life.
