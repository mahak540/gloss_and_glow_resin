Frequently Asked Questions

How long does preservation take?

Most flower and varmala preservation projects take 7-14 days. We'll update you throughout the process and give you a timeline when you inquire.

How much does it cost?

Pricing varies based on the type of preservation, size, and complexity of design. Contact us via WhatsApp for a custom quote based on your specific needs.

How should I send my flowers/varmala?

We'll provide detailed instructions when you contact us. Generally, items should be sent in a clean box with careful packaging to ensure they arrive in good condition.

How long will the resin keepsake last?

Our resin keepsakes are designed to last a lifetime! When kept away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures, they'll retain their beauty for generations.

Can I customize the design?

Absolutely! We offer various design options including different shapes, sizes, colors, and additional decorative elements. Let's discuss your vision when you contact us.
