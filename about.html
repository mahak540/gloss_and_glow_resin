Our Story

Handcraft that Shines with Passion and Purpose

Welcome to Gloss & Glow

Gloss & Glow Resin Art was born from a passion for creating beautiful, handmade products that bring joy to people's lives. What started as a creative hobby has blossomed into a thriving business dedicated to excellence in every piece we craft.

Our journey is rooted in the belief that handmade items carry a special kind of magic. Each resin piece we create tells a story, captures a memory, or becomes a treasured keepsake for someone special.

We work exclusively with premium materials and traditional crafting techniques combined with modern design aesthetics. Our commitment to quality is unwavering — because we believe our customers deserve nothing but the best.

Our Values

  • 🎨 Artistry: Every piece is a work of art crafted with passion
  • ✨ Quality: Premium materials ensure lasting beauty
  • 💝 Authenticity: True handmade, never mass-produced
  • 🤝 Customer Care: Your satisfaction is our priority

Our Journey

2022 - The Beginning

Ashu and Mehak started creating resin art as a hobby, experimenting with designs and techniques in their home studio.

2023 - Growing Passion

Friends and family loved their creations. Orders started coming in, and Gloss & Glow was officially born as a business.

2024 - Expansion

Launched our product line and preservation services. Built a dedicated studio to better serve our growing customer base.

2025 & Beyond

Growing stronger with each customer. Committed to maintaining our quality and handmade values as we scale.
